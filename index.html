Coding Examples

This website was designed for the sake of demonstarting simply coding principles through a variety of different methods, such as conversion calculators, a GPA calculator, and a few simple video games.

Conversion Calculators

All of the conversion calculators were made using free data from the internet. This data was then inputted into simple arithmetic equations to properly convert two different units.

GPA Calculator

The GPA calculator is made by taking a user input of 7 classes, their grade in that class, and each of their type, either Regular, Honors, or AP. Then, it weights the grade based on the class type and calculates their GPA.

Cheesy Clicker

Cheesy Clicker is a project that has been being developed by Eli Mastey for many months. The version shown on this site is an updated version made very recently. Provided you are on a network or device that allows it, this site can be viewed at cheesyclicker.elimastey.com, and will likely be updated periodically through that site. The version seen on this website is static and likely will not be changed.

General Games

In addition to Cheesy Clicker, there are a few simple games created with JavaScript/CSS/HTML principles. These are relatively uncomplicated and will be explained more in-depth when they are shown.
